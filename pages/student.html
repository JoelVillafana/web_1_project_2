Joel Villafana

Client Work

I first got interested in graphic design as a young teenager. While playing video games I discovered various people who posted there designs on there Youtube and Twitter accounts and I told myself I wanted to create that type of art.

I ended up learning photoshop during highschool and made simple design work for friends for their social media accounts. As I got more comfortable I made full rebrand work including a Profile Avatar Banner and various other work people needed like thumbnails.

Since then I have been making tons of Avatars and Banners for people who need their twitter, youtube, or twitch accounts rebranded. It has been about 5-6 years and every day I get a little better with each new design and style.
